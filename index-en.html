📖 About This Learning Media

This media is designed to help students understand the electromagnetic spectrum through interactive visualization. You can explore each type of spectrum (Radio, Microwave, Infrared, Visible Light, Ultraviolet, X-Ray, and Gamma Ray), see how wavelength, frequency, and energy are interrelated, and learn practical applications of each spectrum.

No installation required • Works offline • Responsive for all devices
